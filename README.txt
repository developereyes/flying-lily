# Flying Lily

## Description

Passengers can get coupons for the long waiting times and their knowledge with the help of their friend Flying Lily.
She provides them a discount coupon for the in-flight purchases thus assisting the aircraft carriers to revive their 
lost revenue during the pandemic.
